How It Works

  • Head‑to‑Head: same product, same time limit, best shot advances.
  • Time Limit: One week to complete and submit images.
  • Editing: Basic edits only (exposure, crop, color). No heavy compositing or AI.
  • Judging: Creativity • Composition/Lighting • Product Focus • Brand Fit.
  • Format: Single‑elimination bracket until one champion remains.

Prizes

  • 🏆 Winner: NOMATIC Travel/Photography gear + featured spotlight
  • 🥈 Runner‑Up: NOMATIC prize pack
  • 📸 All Participants: Social media exposure + NOMATIC product seeding

Competition Rules

Round Format

  • Each matchup is head‑to‑head.
  • Both photographers are given the same product.
  • Photos are submitted at the end of the round for judging.

Time Limit

  • Each creator will have one week to complete the task and submit final images.
  • No late submissions will be accepted.

Editing

  • Basic editing is permitted (exposure, cropping, color correction).
  • Heavy compositing or AI generation is not allowed — the image must reflect real product photography.

Judging Criteria

  • Creativity (25%)
  • Composition & Lighting (25%)
  • Product Focus & Clarity (25%)
  • Alignment with Nomatic’s Brand Aesthetic (25%)

Eliminations

  • Winners are chosen by a panel of judges + live audience votes.
  • Single‑elimination bracket format until one Photo Champion remains.

Competitor Guidelines

  • Keep the product safe & clean.
  • Stay on theme — Nomatic is sleek, modern, and built for adventure.
  • Have fun! This is about creativity and community as much as competition.
