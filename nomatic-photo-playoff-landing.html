How It Works

  • Head‑to‑Head: same product, same time limit, best shot advances.
  • Time Limit: 30 minutes per round. No late submissions.
  • Equipment: Bring your own camera/lighting. Tripods & reflectors allowed.
  • Editing: Basic edits only (exposure, crop, color). No heavy compositing or AI.
  • Judging: Creativity • Composition/Lighting • Product Focus • Brand Fit.
  • Format: Single‑elimination bracket until one champion remains.

Prizes

  • Winner: Cash + NOMATIC gear + featured spotlight.
  • Runner‑Up: NOMATIC prize pack.
  • All Participants: Social exposure + product seeding opportunities.
